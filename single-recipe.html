CHICKEN BIRYANI

Chicken biryani is one of the most popular rice dishes and traditionally it is cooked adapting the process of dum pukht, meaning "steam cooked over low fire". The traditional process of Chicken Biryani starts by marinating meat in yogurt along with spices and herbs.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Biryani breakfast simple recipes food

instructions

step 1

Take 2 cups of basumathi rice, and 2kg Chiken with leg pecies

step 2

Take a cooker, put some oil fry onions, tomatos, some spices mix it well until it become brown in color.

step 3

Put chicken and mix it well with powder rice in cooker ,close the lead and wait until two vishels.

ingredients

1 1/2 Basumathi Rice

1kg Chicken

4-5 table spoon oil

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
